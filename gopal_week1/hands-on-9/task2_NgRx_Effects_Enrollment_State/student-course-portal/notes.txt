KEY CONCEPTS
------------
Effects are side-effect handlers only        — reducers remain pure
switchMap cancels in-flight HTTP calls       — use for "latest wins" scenarios
catchError inside inner Observable           — keeps outer Effects stream alive
Cross-slice selectors                        — join state slices without duplicating data
async pipe everywhere                        — automatic subscribe/unsubscribe + CD
